# Privacy Policy for Battery Charge Info

**Last Updated:** December 1, 2025

## Overview

Battery Charge Info is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our app handles information.

## Information We Access

Battery Charge Info accesses the following device information solely to display it to you:

- Battery level and charging status
- Battery voltage and current
- Battery temperature
- Battery health status
- Battery capacity information
- Device model and Android version

## Information We Do NOT Collect

Battery Charge Info does **NOT**:

- Collect any personal information
- Store any data outside of your device
- Send any data to external servers
- Track your usage or behavior
- Use analytics services
- Share any information with third parties
- Require an internet connection
- Contain advertisements or ad tracking

## Data Storage

All battery information displayed in the app is:

- Read directly from your device's system
- Displayed in real-time
- Never stored permanently
- Never transmitted anywhere
- Only accessible while the app is running

The app may store minimal preferences locally on your device (such as the calculated battery capacity) to improve functionality, but this data never leaves your device.

## Permissions

Battery Charge Info does not require any special permissions. The app only reads publicly available battery information that Android provides to all apps.

## Third-Party Services

Battery Charge Info does not use any third-party services, libraries, or SDKs that collect data.

## Children's Privacy

Battery Charge Info does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
